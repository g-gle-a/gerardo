The blue bottle

This is an amazing reduction/oxidation (redox) experiment. You aonly need

  • A bottle
  • a coffe teaspon ofglucose or some glucose solution (from a Pharmacy or Drug Store)
  • Ethylic alcohol from your supermarket
  • Pipe ripper (solid is better) CAUTION: is a burning agent!!!
  • Methylene Blue or a Methylene Blue solution (from a Pharmacy)
