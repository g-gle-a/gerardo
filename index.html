About Me


I am a cuban/portuguese chemist. I have studied my Bachelor Science degree in Chemistry at the Universidad Central "Marta Abreu" de Las Villas, Cuba Then I earned a Master Degree in Organic Chemistry with specialization in Natural Products Chemistry University of La Habana , also in Cuba. Finally, I have obtained my Ph.D. Degree in Materials Science and Engineering at the University of Aveiro , Portugal.

I was Professor of Pharmaceutical and Biorganic Chemistry at the Universidad de Camagüey from 1994 to 2001. In that period I was studying the properties of Medicinal Plants having 4 articles in the standardization of Hibiscus elatus sp.

I am stronly interested in using computer programming to solve chemical problems. This approach is widely known as Chemometrics although I prefer to call it Math4Chem.

When I was sworking on my Ph.D. Degree in Materials Science and Engineering, I made some automation programs for simply DTA and Dielectric measurements in C++. As well as I made some simulations on the crystallization kinetics of ferroelectric Sr2Ta2O9 thin films, cristallized in conventional furnace annealing (CFA) and rapid thermal annealing (RTA).


Another of my interest is the utilization of Arduino boards to make on-line measurements of chemical processes and in that field I was coordinator of a Task of the European FP7 Research Project "SNIFFER"
